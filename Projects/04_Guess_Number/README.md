## 🎮 Game Overview
A sleek, premium, and interactive "Guess the Number" game built with Vanilla JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3. The game generates a random number between 1 and 100 and challenges you to find it!

### ✨ Features
- **Dynamic Logic**: Real-time feedback if your guess is too high or too low.
- **History Tracking**: Keep track of every guess you've made in the current session.
- **Micro-Animations**: Experience smooth transitions and premium UI design.
- **Input Validation**: Ensures you only enter numbers within the valid 1-100 range.

---

## 🚀 How to Play
1. Enter a number between **1 and 100** in the input box.
2. Click the **"Guess it!"** button.
3. Check the feedback:
   - 📈 **Too High**: Your guess is greater than the secret number.
   - 📉 **Too Low**: Your guess is smaller than the secret number.
   - 🎉 **Guessed Correct**: You found the secret number!
4. Check your **Previous Guesses** at the bottom to strategize.
